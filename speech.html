HeyGov and Town Web are sister companies. They're a fast-growing Govtech startup and web agency, respectively, selling software solutions to the local government in the US. About the Role

We're seeking a master Storyteller. It would be somebody to join our marketing team as our lead copywriter. This isn't just any ol' copywriting role, like at other companies. Instead, we're looking for someone who can weave magic with words. Somebody who can transform our innovative digital government solutions into compelling stories. Unlike other companies where they list features and benefits, we want to do things differently. We want the written descriptions of our solutions to resonate with the front-line staff who run municipalities. As our Storyteller, you'll be the narrative architect behind our marketing efforts. Your mission is to go beyond listing features. You'll craft rich, engaging stories that illuminate WHY we build what we build to better describe WHO we're building it for. Your canvas will be the unique world of municipal clerks, PIOs (Public Information Officers), and department heads in local government. Your palette will be the myriad ways our software solutions make their everyday lives easier and more efficient.

You will be responsible for:

• Crafting weekly newsletters Writing thought-provoking blog articles (not just SEO-drivel)
