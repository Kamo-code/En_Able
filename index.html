How does this platform work?

  • Create an En_Able account

    Creating an account for En_Able is essential as it provides users with a personalized experience, enabling them to receive tailored job recommendations and skill development programs based on their interests and abilities. It allows users to track their employment journey, including job applications, interviews, and training progress, while also granting access to exclusive resources and stay updated on job openings and industry trends. Additionally, an account enhances security and data privacy, ensuring that personal information remains protected while allowing users to manage their privacy settings.

  • Fill in the questionnaire

    Filling in a detailed questionnaire is crucial for En_Able as it allows the platform to personalize job recommendations, skill development programs, and accessibility features based on individual needs. It helps match users with relevant opportunities, training, and companies, ensuring a tailored experience.

  • Apply for relevant jobs and upskill

    Applying for relevant jobs and upskilling on En_Able is vital as it increases employment opportunities and helps users stay competitive in the job market. By continuously learning new skills, users can match industry demands, access better career prospects, and improve their financial stability.

Success stories

Julie's story

I'm Julie, and I have intellectual and developmental differences that made finding the right job challenging. The employment platform connected me with a supportive employer who understood my needs and provided a nurturing environment. Now, I'm thriving in my role, showing everyone that with the right support, anyone can succeed.

Joshua's story

I'm Joshua, and I live with autism. I've always been passionate about swimming, but my fear of water held me back. My dream was to become a lifeguard. With the support of En_Able I received the necessary training and job reccommondations. Today, I'm a confident lifeguard, saving lives and inspiring others with my determination.

Sarah's story

I'm Sarah, and I was born with a congenital limb difference, but that has never defined me. Known for my contagious enthusiasm, I was determined to find a job that matched my skills and personality. The platform helped me secure a full-time job with benefits. Now, I'm not only excelling at my job but also spreading positivity and hope to everyone around me.
